The Story

Where the name comes from

Faery Tech started as a gamertag — a little whimsical, a little techy, completely me. But the name carries something real.

Growing up in Alabama, I chased fireflies in the summer and genuinely believed fairies lived in the mushroom rings at the edge of the yard. I still get excited when I see mushrooms now. That sense of wonder — that the world is full of hidden magic — never left me.

Fairies remind me of my mom, of childhood, of a time when the world felt full of possibility. And that's exactly what I want to give small business owners: the feeling that anything is possible, that the right tools can transform their situation, that someone showed up and made the hard thing easy.

Magic isn't fantasy. It's what happens when the right person shows up at the right moment with the right tools.

The Founder

Mission Statement

The world is moving fast.
No one should get left behind.

Technology shouldn't make us lonelier, more isolated, or obsolete. In the right hands, it's the great equalizer — a tool that can lift a struggling entrepreneur, connect a neighborhood, and fund a cleaner, kinder world.

Faery Tech exists to put those tools in the right hands.

We build websites, digital systems, and online presence for small businesses, nonprofits, and people just getting started — regardless of what they can afford. Our sliding-scale pricing and community hardship rates mean that a business idea and a dream are enough to get started.

Every site we build joins the Faery Tech network — a growing digital ecosystem where local businesses connect with each other and their communities. Ad revenue across the entire network is reinvested into our charitable mission: supporting animal shelters, eco-friendly initiatives, tree planting programs, and free web services for those who need them most.

We partner only with brands we believe in. We advertise only what we'd recommend to a friend.

Because profit and purpose were never opposites.
